    How Prod makes money

    Prod is designed to make your time on the internet happier and more productive.

    That's why Prod will never: show ads, sell your data, bug you to upgrade, or annoy you.

    For now, Prod's only source of revenue is affiliate links.


    Affiliate Links Explained

    Affiliate links appear throughout the website, and to a lesser extent this extension.

    Clicking on one of these links will send you to a product, usually a book. If you choose to buy one of these products, Prod will receive a small percentage of the profits.

    Prod doesn't have a special deal with any of these products, or any vested interest other than the money made if you buy them.

    If there's a better product that doesn't earn Prod any money, a link to that product will be displayed instead.
